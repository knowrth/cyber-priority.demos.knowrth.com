Saviynt

We help enterprises modernize IAM programs, reduce onboarding time by up to 70%, and achieve regulatory compliance efficiently.

Saviynt Enterprise Identity Cloud (EIC) is a cloud-native Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) platform with advanced risk analytics, SoD controls, and cloud PAM capabilities.

    Our Capabilities:
  • • IGA Deployment: Implement identity lifecycle management, access request workflows, and certifications.
  • • Application Access Governance: Configure SoD analysis, risk-based access controls, and audit reporting.
  • • Cloud PAM: Deploy Just-in-Time privileged access for critical applications.
  • • Integration Expertise: Connect ERP systems (SAP, Oracle), cloud apps, and ServiceNow for unified governance.
  • • Automation & Analytics: Leverage AI/ML-based risk scoring and policy-driven automation.
